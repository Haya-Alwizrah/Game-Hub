📜 Game Rules

  • The game is played on a 3x3 board with 3 pawns for each player.
  • Pawns can move one square forward if the square is empty.
  • Pawns can capture an enemy pawn diagonally forward (left or right).
  • How to Win: Reach the opponent's starting row, capture all opponent's pawns, or leave them with no legal moves left!
